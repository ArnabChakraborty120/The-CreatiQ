Saint Visage x Invisalign

Service

Project Keys

Creative Directing

Description

In 2023, Saint Visage collaborated with Invisalign to offer exclusive deals on invisible flexible aligners to their customers. The project aimed to create a buzz around this partnership, highlighting the benefits of Invisalign’s aligners through engaging and informative marketing campaigns. My role as the creative designer was pivotal in bringing this collaboration to life.

My Contribution:

I played a key role in this collaboration, ensuring that both brands were effectively represented and the marketing message was clear and compelling. My contributions included:
Creative Design: Designed a series of visually appealing social media posts and stories to promote the collaboration. The designs were crafted to resonate with the target audience and align with the aesthetics of both brands.
Marketing Campaigns: Collaborated closely with the marketing teams of both Saint Visage and Invisalign to develop and execute marketing strategies. This included brainstorming sessions, content planning, and campaign execution.
Content Creation: Produced a variety of digital content, including graphics, animations, and promotional materials, to highlight the benefits of Invisalign aligners and the exclusive deals offered through Saint Visage.
Brand Consistency: Ensured that all creative outputs maintained the brand identities of both Saint Visage and Invisalign. This involved adhering to brand guidelines and creating a cohesive visual narrative.

The marketing campaign successfully communicated the value of the collaboration between Saint Visage and Invisalign, generating significant interest and engagement from customers. The visually striking and informative content helped in driving awareness and sales for the exclusive aligner deals, showcasing the effectiveness of the partnership.
